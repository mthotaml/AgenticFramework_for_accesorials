# Demo Guide

Use this guide when recording or presenting the Agentic Accessorial Auditor.

## Quick Talk Track

This app reviews extra freight invoice fees called accessorial charges. Instead of only showing the charges, it uses agents to check the contract rules, inspect shipment evidence, explain the decision, and draft the next action.

## Suggested Flow

1. Open the dashboard.
2. Explain the top metrics:
   - Invoice exposure: total freight invoice dollars under review
   - Disputed: amount the system thinks may be wrong
   - Charges: number of accessorial fees being reviewed
   - High risk: shipments needing attention
3. Click a shipment in the shipment queue.
4. Explain that the queue is the worklist of freight loads requiring review.
5. Select a charge tab, such as detention, lumper, layover, or TONU.
6. Walk through the Auditor Agent recommendation.
7. Point out invoice line, allowed amount, and challenge amount.
8. Show the findings and explain that these are the reasons behind the recommendation.
9. Show the Evidence Timeline and explain that the agent cites specific shipment events.
10. Show Multi-Agent Review:
    - Contract Reviewer: what is allowed
    - Shipment Investigator: what happened
    - Billing Analyst: was it billed correctly
    - Final Adjudicator: what should we do
11. Click Generate dispute packet.
12. Explain that the app turns the decision into a carrier-facing action.
13. Ask the Ops Copilot: `What evidence supports this dispute?`
14. Close by saying the agent layer turns a static accessorial POC into an active audit workflow.

## Non-Domain Explanation

A shipment is one delivery trip.

An accessorial charge is an extra fee on top of the normal shipping cost.

The app helps answer: should we pay the fee, dispute it, deny it, or ask for more proof?

The agents act like a small review team:

- One checks the contract.
- One checks what happened during the delivery.
- One checks the invoice math.
- One checks whether data is missing.
- One makes the final recommendation.
- One drafts the dispute.
- One answers user questions.
